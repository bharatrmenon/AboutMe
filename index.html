The 8-Year Ascent

In 2016, I moved to the United States to pursue a master's degree in Innovation Management & Entrepreneurship at Brown University. My background was in engineering—I earned a master's degree in electrical engineering from the National University of Singapore and spent years solving complex problems in green energy. But I wanted to go beyond technical expertise and deepen my understanding of business strategy.

In 2017, I joined Pentagon Safety Equipment as a Business Operations Engineer. When the global pandemic hit in 2020, I founded and led our e-commerce division from scratch. The results were transformative: digital sales skyrocketed from $10,000 to over $1,000,000 within three years. Today, e-commerce accounts for 90% of our total sales.
